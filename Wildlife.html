Wildlife

  • Wildlife refers to undomesticated animal species found worldwide.
  • Diverse species inhabit various ecosystems from deserts to oceans.
  • Common groups include birds, mammals, reptiles, and insects.
  • Protecting habitats is vital for maintaining a balanced planet.
  • Conservation efforts help save endangered species and biodiversity.
