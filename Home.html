Welcome to Our Nature World

Animals are an essential part of our planet’s ecosystems. Biodiversity refers to the variety of living species on Earth, including animals, plants, and microorganisms, that maintain balance in nature.

This website is about exploring animals and understanding the importance of biodiversity.
